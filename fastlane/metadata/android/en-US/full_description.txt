<p>E-mail client for the Android platform.</p>
<p>IMAP, POP, and SMTP via SSL/TLS, with AES/PGP support.</p>

<p>Features:</p>

<ul>
<li>* Animate a smooth transition between visual contexts.</li>
<li>* Automatically convert texts from their declared character encoding to UTF-8.</li>
<li>* Download your full email messages (with attachments inside).</li>
<li>* Download an individual attachment.</li>
<li>* Display server certificates used in the last connection.</li>
<li>* Keep track of your unread messages.</li>
<li>* Notify with sound of new messages, per user choice.</li>
<li>* Notify with device vibration of new messages, per user choice.</li>
<li>* Work with OpenPGP messages.</li>
<li>* Verify hostnames, if not self-signed certificates.</li>
</ul>
